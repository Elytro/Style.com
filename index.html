An Easy Remedy Against Gastroenteritis

Gastroenteritis (also known as gastro, gastric flu, and stomach flu, although unrelated to influenza) is inflammation of the gastrointestinal tract, involving both the stomach and small intestine (see also gastritis and enteritis), resulting in acute diarrhea.

The inflammation is most often caused by viral infections, less commonly by bacteria or their toxins, parasites, or adverse reactions to diet or medication.

Worldwide, inadequate treatment of gastroenteritis kills 5 to 8 million people per year and is a leading cause of death among infants and children under 5.

Recipe

  • 1 liter water
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon bicarbonate (this ingredient is essential!)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Drink up to 2 liters per day, although 2-3 glasses should be sufficient to feel improvement.
